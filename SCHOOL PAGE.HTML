Welcome to DMPS...........
The School is located by the side of NH 33, 25 kms. away from Jamshedpur on the way to Ranchi. The Bus stop is HUMID/ SAI BABA TEMPLE. The school is 600 m away from the Highway. How to Reach : Approach from Jamshedpur Board Jamshedpur Ranchi bus from Mango Bus Station Jamshedpur. Get down in HUMID/ SAI BABA TEMPLE. Approach from Ranchi/ Chandil Avail bus from Ranchi / Chandil to Jamshedpur via NH 330. Get down in Humid.
